# Allocations/Unlock Schedule (GeM)

![CollarQuest a Metaverse Play2Earn Ecosystem](../../../.gitbook/assets/CollarQuest-SM.png)

| Reserves             | 1,000,000,000     | 20%      | DAO Oversight                                         |

| Minting fee          | 25,00,000         | 0.5%     | Issued                                                |
